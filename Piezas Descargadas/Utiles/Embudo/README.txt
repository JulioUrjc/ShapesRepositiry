# Summary

<h3>This is a customizer to generate funnels parametrically.</h3>

Version 7.0 available. 
Although it is a remix (thanks Salvador Richter), it expands the original project substantially. 

Customize the funnel with the following parameters:

***Size and shape***
- **diameters**: external diameters at three locations (top, middle, bottom);
- overall **height**;
- **wall** thickness;
- **neck ratio**: the higher the parameter the longer the "neck" of the funnel. At 1, the neck is one half the overall length, at 3 the neck is 5/6 of the overall length;
- **squish ratio**: sometimes you need funnel with a "squished" elliptical section. (if >1, the "diameters" are the major axes of the ellipse);

***Optional features***
- **handle length**: generates a handle if this parameter is not zero, the larger the parameter, the longer the handle;
- **add vent**: generates supports that allows air to escape around the neck;
- **high wall**: if selected, the well of the funnel has a steep cylindrical section before narrowing;
- **slanted tip**: cuts the tip at a 35degree angle.

Future possible developments include adding an offset for hard to reach filling jobs.
